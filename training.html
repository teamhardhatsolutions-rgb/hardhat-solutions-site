Straight talk on certification Per OSHA 29 CFR 1910.178, powered industrial truck operators (including skid steers and telehandlers) must complete both formal instruction AND a hands-on practical evaluation performed by a qualified person on the actual equipment at the worksite. Our online courses fulfill the formal instruction requirement. They are not a substitute for the required hands-on evaluation — that portion has to happen at your site, on your machine, with your evaluator.
